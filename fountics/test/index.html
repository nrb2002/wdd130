FOUNTICS

We support green innovative IT solutions designed to improve life while saving the planet

Our partners

We build professional capacities of the active and potential workforce, including students, researchers, and scientists.

We foster a culture of innovation by popularizing the basics of computer science to all segments of the population.

Fountics is an applied information technology research center that aims to build a long-term strategic development plan for the Democratic Republic of Congo. By focusing on real-life case studies, we add socio-economic value to scientific and technological advances by finding solutions to the day-to-day problems for local businesses, communities, and individuals.

Our strategy is threefold:

  • Support innovative and ecological IT solutions designed to improve the living conditions of local populations;
  • Build professional capacities of the active and potential workforce (students, researchers, scientists, Etc.);
  • Foster a culture of innovation by popularizing the basics of computer science to all segments of the population.
