Research

Our Research Center is leading an approach to popularize computer science among all segments of the population with a focus on youth. We collaborate with local and international partners to develop new technologies and provide an enabling environment for local scientific researchers to find sustainable solutions to society's daily problems. In addition to these broad ambitions, the center shares what it learns so that its information can improve the lives of local people. We conduct research through real-life testing and experiments to advance the digitization and automation of systems country-wide, including using renewable energy. We develop and fund technologies that will enable future exploration and benefits community life.
